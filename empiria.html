Empiria

Interactive Learning | Editorial Design | AI Exploration | 2025

Empiria is a hybrid project that makes artificial intelligence accessible through editorial design and play. By combining a book and a board game with four game modes, it turns AI into something clear—explained through experience, played by everyone.

The Empiria Kit is built on the concept of "Think like an AI": analyze, connect, generate, and discover what it means to think through logic, connections, and artificial intuition. It includes a set of instructions, three decks of cards (Token, Calculation, and Pixel), and four game modes, offering a creative and interactive way to explore computational thinking.
