Il Divo

Video Editing | 2024

Il divo is an experimental project dealing with a reworking of the promo of paolo sorrentino's film Il Divo. The main character is Giulio Andreotti, former Prime Minister of the Italian Republic and one of the leading figures of the Christian Democrats.
What was his role in the 20th century?

The treatment and choice of scenes are driven by a social and political critique.
How in the 20th century do Mafia, the Church, and politics have a common thread connecting them?

The rights to the all images/videos related to the video editing project of the film DIVO by Paolo Sorrentino are owned by ISIA Rome. Their use is permitted only for academic purposes.
