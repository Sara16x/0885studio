Insight into grids

Motion graphics | 2023

Insight into grids is an experimental project that explores the laws of proximity, symmetry, and closure.
The grid, a composition of vertical and horizontal lines constantly changing in thickness and size, is the medium by four laws of gestalt are explained.

Proximity

Elements close to each other are perceived as a group.

Simmetry

Symmetrical elements are perceived as a part of the same group.

Closure

Incomplete elements are filled by our visual perception.

Common fate

Elements that follow the same direction are perceived as a group.
